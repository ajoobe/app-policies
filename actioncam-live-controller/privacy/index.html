Privacy Policy

ActionCam Live Controller · Last updated: July 15, 2026

Package: com.ajoobe.actioncamlivecontroller

1. Introduction

This privacy policy describes how ActionCam Live Controller (“the App”) handles user data. The App is a remote control tool for compatible action cameras that enables live video streaming to RTMP-compatible destinations.

The developer does not currently operate a backend server, cloud service, or analytics platform for this App. All user data described in this document is stored locally on the user's device unless otherwise stated.

The App is developed by an individual developer and is not affiliated with DJI, Osmo, Streamrun, Twitch, YouTube, Kick, or any other camera or streaming service provider.

2. What the App Does

The App connects to a compatible action camera (currently tested with DJI Osmo Action 4) via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to configure the camera's Wi-Fi connection and instruct the camera to begin streaming video to an RTMP destination specified by the user.

The App itself does not capture, process, or transmit video or audio. The streaming video is transmitted directly from the camera to the RTMP destination over Wi-Fi; the App sends only control commands to the camera.

3.2 Data Passed to the User's Camera (via BLE)

The App sends control commands to the user's camera over Bluetooth Low Energy. This data does not leave the local Bluetooth connection between the phone and the camera:

DataDestinationCircumstance
Wi-Fi configuration (SSID, password)User's camera via BLEUser sets up camera Wi-Fi
RTMP destination info (server URL, stream key)User's camera via BLEUser starts a live stream
Stream control commands (start, stop, preset)User's camera via BLEUser starts or stops streaming

The camera then uses the provided RTMP destination info to connect directly to the user's chosen RTMP streaming service. The App is not involved in the video/audio transmission between the camera and the RTMP service.

3.3 Data Not Collected by the Developer

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, address) is collected or requested.
  • No location data is collected, stored, or transmitted for tracking purposes. (Android requires Location permission for Wi-Fi scanning; see Section 5. The App does not read, store, or transmit the device's location.)
  • No advertising ID or similar device identifiers are accessed by the App.
  • No account registration is required.
  • No data is uploaded to any server operated by the developer.
  • No analytics, crash reports, telemetry, or usage statistics are collected or transmitted by the App.
  • No cloud sync is used. All data remains on the device only.

7. User Control

Users can manage their data as follows:

  • Delete saved Wi-Fi networks: Remove individual Wi-Fi profiles from the Settings screen.
  • Delete saved RTMP destinations: Remove individual stream destinations from the Settings screen.
  • Clear all app data: Use Android's "Clear storage" option in System Settings → Apps → ActionCam Live Controller → Storage. This removes all locally stored data.
  • Uninstall the App: Removes all locally stored data.
  • Revoke permissions: Use Android's App Permissions settings.

8. Data Retention

Data remains on the device until the user deletes it via the methods described in Section 7 or uninstalls the App.

9. Children's Privacy
