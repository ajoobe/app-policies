About the App

ActionCam Live Controller is a remote control tool for compatible action cameras. It connects to your camera via Bluetooth, sets up Wi-Fi, and instructs the camera to start a live RTMP stream to a destination you choose.

The app does not capture or transmit video. Your camera streams directly to your RTMP destination over Wi-Fi. The app sends only control commands to the camera.

Currently tested with DJI Osmo Action 4 (Direct Mode).

Basic Troubleshooting

Permissions

  • The app shows a permission card on first launch. Tap "Grant Required Permissions" and allow each prompt.
  • Required permissions: Bluetooth (camera discovery and connection), Location (required by Android for Wi-Fi scanning — the app does not track your location), Wi-Fi state, Internet.
  • If permissions are denied, the permission card will remain until all required permissions are granted.

Camera Connection

  • Ensure your camera is powered on and within Bluetooth range (~10 metres).
  • Tap "Find Camera" on the Stream Control screen.
  • Select your camera from the list. Status should change to "Connected".
  • If the camera does not appear, ensure it is not already connected to another device via the DJI Mimo app.

Wi-Fi Selection

  • Tap the Wi-Fi row on the Stream Control screen.
  • Select your Wi-Fi network from the scan results or choose a saved network.
  • If using your phone's mobile hotspot, select the hotspot SSID.
  • Enter the Wi-Fi password if prompted. Passwords are stored using encrypted storage.

RTMP Destination Setup

  • Tap the destination row, then "Add Destination".
  • Enter a Name (a label you will recognise), a Server URL from your streaming service, and a Stream Key from your streaming service.
  • The stream key is masked by default for privacy. Tap the eye icon to verify what you entered.
  • Stream keys are redacted in normal display and logs where practical.

Stream Not Starting?

  • Ensure the camera is connected.
  • Ensure Wi-Fi is configured and the camera has joined the network.
  • Ensure a destination is selected (name + server URL + stream key).
  • Ensure a quality preset is selected.
  • Check your streaming service dashboard to confirm the stream is visible.

Safety Notes

  • Do not operate the app while driving. If testing in a vehicle, have a passenger handle the device.
  • Do not share your stream key. Anyone with your stream key can stream to your channel. Redact or blur stream keys in any screenshots or recordings you share.
  • Check your streaming platform's terms of service to ensure third-party streaming tools are permitted.

Known Limitations

  • Only tested with DJI Osmo Action 4. Other cameras may not be compatible.
  • Relay Mode is not available in the current release (experimental, future feature).
  • No in-app purchases or donations (Play Billing not implemented).
  • Hotspot / mobile data streaming is in testing and may have limitations.
